Getting Started

  1. Grant HealthKit Permission: Open StepWise and allow access to Steps, Walking + Running Distance, and Active Energy data when prompted
  2. Set Your Daily Goal: Customize your daily step goal in Settings (default: 10,000 steps)
  3. Track Your Progress: Watch your beautiful circular progress ring fill up as you move throughout the day
  4. View Your History: Explore weekly charts and historical trends in the History tab

Does StepWise work with Apple Watch?
Yes! StepWise reads data from Apple Health, which automatically syncs with Apple Watch. Any steps recorded on your watch will appear in StepWise.
Can I change my daily step goal?
Absolutely! Go to Settings and adjust your daily goal to any value that works for you. The default is 10,000 steps, but you can customize it to match your fitness level.
Is my health data shared or stored online?
Never! StepWise is completely privacy-focused. All your health data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. We don't have user accounts, logins, or cloud storage.
